Web and Software design at InVision

In my 3 years of apprenticeship, I worked in many different areas of software development and web design within the company. Here you can see a small overview of my designs, which made it to production

injixo csv-importer UI

Behaviour after file was picked

Discription

This is the first iteration on production, which allows customers to upload their historical call data. Together with an UX-Designer, I started to interview customers and co-workers which worked in the same area before. Then we created a User Journey for the targeted customer group and evaluated it together with some customers. Afterwards we came up with some wireframes and a paper prototype to test the desirable functionality. Since everyone was fine with the outcome, I could start to mock-up the whole component and its user-flow.
